Our Story — How M² Began

Manish Mishra and Madhvi Rai — both seasoned educators from India's top coaching ecosystem — came together with a shared frustration: premium education in India was either too impersonal, too crowded, or too expensive for the average family.


Starting from Delhi/NCR and expanding pan-India through online delivery, M² Learning Hub was built to offer Aakashian-quality teaching in an intimate, personalised format — where a student is never just a roll number.


Every class at M² is an experience: concepts that connect, doubts that get cleared the same day, and parents who always know what's happening inside the classroom.
